Description

The Lilliputian Runner™ is a skill-based platformer, where the player's goal is to find anddestroyevery lilliput character walking around the levels.
Player accomplishes this by changing the player character's color to the same as the lilliput player is colliding with is.
Colliding with a lilliput while not being of the same color as they are, will destroy the player character.

Player's color starts on each level asgrey, and will change the following way; colliding with anothergreylilliput changes it toorange, then colliding with anotherorangelilliput changes it toblue, and colliding with anotherbluelilliput changes the color back to beinggrey.


- The game offers multiple hours of fun and challenging gameplay with over 50 levels to conquer.

- Many levels offer alternative routes and methods to complete them, so use your wits and skills to figure out the best way to approach each of them.

- In-game checkpoints and slow motion gameplay added with update 2.0!

Description

Made by Dragon Jump Team, Arena of Cube is a cube sandbox competition game providing a variety of game modes and sandbox gameplay. Choosing different heroes and occupations to participate in the PVP battle zone, making good use of your cubes and skills to defeat your opponent and join the top ladder list!

At present, our existing game modes are BedWars, SkyWars and SnowWars.

We will release at least one new gameplay every month. Of course, we are delighted to offer you better ideas.



Three major game modes:

SkyWars:

Rules: the rules of SkyWar are the simplest. Players will be randomly born on a small island at the very beginning. There will be random items of resources in the battlefields. The player can defeat all opponents to win the game but can not be resurrected after being defeated.

Skills: the surrounding islands produce supplies and synthetic materials, and players could avoid disputes and accumulate more resources. The central island produces more advanced armor and weapons.



BedWars:

Rules: protect your own bed and try to destroy the enemy's bed. Player can be resurrected after being defeated as long as his bed is not destroyed.

Tips: pick up diamonds on the resource island, and use diamonds to upgrade skill. Player who defeats the central island will win the game. Please note that the bed is fragile! The most traditional way is to use a square.



SnowWars:

Rules: get snowballs and defeat the opponents by throwing snowballs, and get the highest score and become winner after the game counts down.

Skills: occupy the high platform to get unlimited snowballs, having more advantages in score.

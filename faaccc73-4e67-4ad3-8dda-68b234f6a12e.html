Description

Escape from Poalet (Prologue) is a point & click 3d graphic adventure game for one player.
Some of the game mechanics are different from the conventional ones and in this case, the typical inventory has been eliminated.
The game is set in medieval times and follows a very nice and pleasant aesthetic.
Help our protagonist to escape from Poalet while discovering all its secrets and all its characters and enjoy their dialogues with a touch of humor and the voices dubbed in Spanish.

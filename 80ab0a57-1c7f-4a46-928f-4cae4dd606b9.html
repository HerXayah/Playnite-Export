Description

Based on the old fps, Terra Atlantis add his own kind of originality with enhanced graphisme and gamplay mechanisme.

More than 5 levels playable, thigtly designed to guide you in an immersive universe created by gods.

With the cryo x32, weapons of gods, you will never need to reload the ammo.

Jump, run and shoot and kill everything that stand in your way.

Fight against The TAB, the FILE, the Hunter and many more ennemies... and be sure to defeat the hand of god.

Find the secrets levels and upgrade your own gun.

And be sure to appreciate the original soundtrack made by BABEFAKE and SAMSUNG WATER.

Description

Ever wanted visit one of Dracul's castles? Wondered how do they keep changing? Maybe experience non-euclidean horrors and confusion first hand?
You're in luck!

For the first time in history, we're opening Zlowrogowo Castle to the public!

Fast Paced Fps Action

Run and gun everything that moves. Take monsters from safe distance with your trusty revolver, go close and personal with double barrel shotgun or even use enemies against each other. Go explore off the main path and you may find artifacts that make you stronger, just be careful not to get lost...

Both purely procedural and handmade locations

The castle and it's surrounding grounds always change and most of areas you'll see only once.
Few rooms resists chaos, but this doesn't mean they're safe...

Variety of game modes

  • Campaign mode - tackle main path that leads deeper into the castle and face fearsome guardians at the end of each zone.
  • Arcade mode in which you will be given limited time to go as far - and score as much - as possible.
  • Custom map mode - play around with map generator and create your own levels.

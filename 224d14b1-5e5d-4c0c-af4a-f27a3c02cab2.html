Description

Paintball War is an addictive first-person shooter in which one of two opposing teams wins!
The game is based on a technical sport called Paintball. Starting the game in one of the modes, you must destroy the opposing team, winning them on points while being in equal conditions! The intense battle in Paintball War takes place both in small specialized areas and in huge areas in forests, settlements or even sewers!
In the game you will find various paintball markers, as well as many maps, which will provide you with a large selection of locations to play!

Description

Arrow is alocal twin-stick shootergame, playable from2 to 4 players. Gather weapons and bonus to blow up your friends in a randomly generated scrolling map ! Multiple game modes are available to show who is the best :

  • InLast Alive, stay careful and be the last standing to win the match !
  • InFree for All, claim 3 kills before your opponents to win the match !
  • InTeam Fight, choose your side and empty the enemy team lives to win the match !

A fun game to play with your friends on party !

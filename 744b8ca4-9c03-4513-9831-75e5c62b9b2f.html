Description

Heroic City is an impactverse - an online gaming world with the purpose of generating real-world impact! join one of our sponsors teams and play dozens of action-pack party games to unlock new skins, decorations and impact items. Hatch impact items into objects you can send to the real world to plant trees, remove plastic waste from oceans and offset carbon from the atmosphere. The choice is yours - the community together decides which issues we focus on. Be bold, be Heroic!

Every tree you plant in Heroic City will be planted to the real world by our partner Ecologi.

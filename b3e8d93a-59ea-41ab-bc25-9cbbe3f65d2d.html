Description

This is the interactive music video of the song Cold Burn by the band Cult of Luna. Help the lost light escape those dark tornados and guide it through the mysterious landscapes, all the way to the Mother Light at the core of the maze.

Explore this surreal world, fight the tornados or see how many more lost lights you can find and bring with you home to the end of the maze. Enough lights collected in each part of the maze will unlock artworks at the end of the experience. How many lights can you find?

The music is adaptive to navigating the game, so every experience will render a unique version of the song.

Description

Tekling is a challenging body-shifting 2D platformer. Jump and dodge your way through 70+ levels of frenetic gameplay of other challenging platformers like Super Meat boy and Megaman.

Mild mannered Jack the pizza delivery boy's life changed forever when he was infected with super powered nanites. Now with the aid of exotic particles he can turn into Tekling, a mighty robot hero who must collect these strange particles to save the populace. In each area Jack must use his pathetic jumping and running skills to collect three exotic particles which turns him into the super powered Tekling who can jump twice as high, use amazing abilities and even jump and climb walls. Avoid deadly obstacles, collect the power-ups and become a hero.

Tekling was built by adults on the autism spectrum at the nonPareil Institute.

Description

Visually detailed art game with fun way to move
3rd person adventure art game with a lot of details and realistic graphics as you move around with a rope.

Mysterious story about the ancient spirit world
Step on a journey with a dragon to learn about the story behind the mountain.

Mount Serenity: Guardian of the Spirits takes you to an ancient spirit world, where you play as a lost spirit, Mio. You will meet a dragon called Shan who needs your help. As you take the soul towards the top of the mountain, you will move around with a swinging rope that you can also use to drag and move around boxes. To be able to move between areas, you need to collect glowing runestones to open the gates separating them. Starting from a dying forest, you will come across with various beautiful areas while reaching the top in this visually pleasing and calming art game with enjoyable music and sounds effects.

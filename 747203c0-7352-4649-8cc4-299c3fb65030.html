Description

Bog Lord is a casual turn-based strategy game about commanding monstrous units and conquering terrain. Outsmart your friends in short local multiplayer matches and claim victory in the struggle between the chaotic bog and deathly fowl.

Key features:

  • Unique Pattern-based Summoning • Determine when to expand or sacrifice your terrain to increase the efficiency with which you can conquer the board.
  • Territory Control • Use your unit's abilities to spread your terrain and create further opportunities while preventing your rival from being able to fight back.
  • Simplicity and Flexibility • A simple game system with a lot of flexibility in terms of action order, creating a large amount of depth.
  • Bog vs Fowl • Reclaim the land for the wild-natured bog or try to snuff out all life by commanding the eerie fowl. This conflict is complimented by colourful hand-drawn visuals.

Developed by students at the Breda University of Applied Sciences

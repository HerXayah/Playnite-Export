Freakshow came out originally August 2017! This is to celebrate the anniversary of the project!

You go visit the local circus and he takes you. What will you discover?

-Achievements
-Original soundtrack!
-Inventory system
-Better voice acting
-And more!


Come play a few puzzles and enjoy the mini games!

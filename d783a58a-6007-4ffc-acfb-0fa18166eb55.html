Description

Battlegrounds takes the best part of roguelites, tower-defense, strategy and action genres and fuses them to create an enticing gameplay experience with easy to understand mechanics.
The player must defend his home base from enemy tanks by quickly brainstorming good strategies, unlocking and upgrading machines and making the best use of every new environment present.



  • Highly Replayable
  • Awesome soundtracks
  • Unlock and upgrade special vehicles
  • Explore various strategies and playstyles
  • Unlock achievements

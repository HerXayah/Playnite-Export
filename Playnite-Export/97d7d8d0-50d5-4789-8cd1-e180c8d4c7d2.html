Description

NOARA REVOLUTIONIZES THE TURN-BASED GAME

Noara : The Conspiracy is a new hybrid strategy game that offers a unique experience, a mix of RTS, turn-based and card games.
A brand new genre, named MOCA, for Multiplayer Online Chess Arena. Thanks to a unique mechanic, Noara revolutionizes turn-based games by offering the possibility to play in real time during your opponent's turn!
The time when you had to endure enemy assaults without doing anything is over!
In Noara you can heal, dodge, block, retaliate, stun, attack and much more... All during your opponent's turn! Awesome, isn't it?!

THE BLOODY KHINAS MODE

Goal of the game - In this 1 vs 1 mode, you must reach a total of 3000 Khinas before your opponent to win a game. To do this, you have two options:
• The first is to secure the chests that appear randomly on the battlefield and take their contents.
• The second option is to eliminate enemy units which will bring back an amount of Khinas proportional to their level.

Note that it is also possible to win the game by killing all enemy units.

Build your Deck - Before launching a game, you will be able to create your army of 6 champions from the 25 currently playable units. Don't hesitate to build and test several decks in order to find the synergies that fit best with your play style.
You'll see that certain army compositions can create devastating combos!
For those who prefer to delay Theorycraft for later, you can choose the decks proposed by default and have fun without waiting!

Equip your units - Before you step onto the battlefield, you need to arm yourself! You will have to choose 6 items that can be equipped by your units to give them important bonuses.
To make the right decisions, base your choices on your own composition but also on the enemy's army in order to counter efficiently the opponent's strategy.

The Treasure Hunt - You will notice that chests will appear randomly in the arena during the game. It is important to grab them before your opponent in order to collect their contents, as they contain precious treasures.

Indeed, each chest will offer Khinas, but also random rare items, and will allow from time to time to call for reinforcement units.
As you can guess, the chests are crucial to victory. So make sure to prevent your opponent from getting them.

Manhunt - In war, violence is often the answer. If you don't feel like chasing chests, killing all your opponent's units is a good way to win. But beware, without strategy, violence is nothing.

A COMPETITIVE GAME

Noara: The Conspiracy mixes the thinking of turn-based strategy games with the speed of real-time games. This unique gameplay mechanic brings great replayability and will make each battle a real tactical challenge.

Noara has all the characteristics of a competitive game and our wish is to offer our players the possibility to participate in many tournaments and to live great Esportives experiences!

AN ATYPICAL STORY

Noara is a vast heroic-fantasy universe that extends far beyond the video game. If two races fight each other in our first game, there are many more in the Lands of Noara and if you want to know more, we invite you to read the first volume entitled "Noara - The Last Moon".

Description

Smart Factory Tycoon is a tycoon management game about running your dream factory with robots. Design your dream factory, plan all steps of production, research new technologies and become a tycoon!

Machines & Robots

  • Purchase new machinery and unlock advanced machines to produce every type of products in the market
  • Purchase new robots with varying colors and watch them coming to your factory by different transportation options: balloon, mine cart, boat, parachute, PWC or bus
  • Use the skill points to level up your robots (Movement speed, processing speed, charge capacity, oil capacity)
  • Purchase chargers, super chargers, wireless chargers, and lubricators for your robots rooms
  • Upgrade your machines, solar panels, wind turbines, chargers and lubricators for faster production and increased efficiency
  • Fully automated machine-maintenance by drones

Production & Market

  • Plan all steps of the production
  • Purchase high quality raw material to produce healthy and durable products
  • Analyze the market to be aware of trending products
  • Check the general market categories (Basic-Medium-Master) and Special Orders
  • Increase your health, durability and sustainability levels to be eligible for some products in the market

Sustainability & Economy

  • Purchase wind turbines and solar panels to reduce electricity costs and increase your sustainability
  • Plant trees in your gardens to be a sustainable factory
  • Earn more money and XP to take the first place in the leaderboard
  • Do the math and be aware of your expenses! Don't go bankrupt!

Invest & Research & Development

  • Research new technologies to improve your factory
  • Purchase new buildings and deliver your products by rail, sea, and air
  • Build new rooms for robots and production
  • Place your product zones and define their types as general or export
  • Achieve milestones to earn more XP and level-up your factory

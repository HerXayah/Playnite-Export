Episode No. 2

A jump scare as the foundation of horror

Episode No. 1

Dying Light was released in 2015. Combining an open world with parkour, it fully embraced the philosophy of efficient movement without boundaries. From jumping over zombies to traveling by rooftop, the game is made to keep you running non-stop. This is the first and only video game that recreates the kind of freedom parkour brings to reality. The result is the best movement mechanics in an open world game. So, we decided to make a clip about it. With your help, of course. You will be the one to show people freedom.

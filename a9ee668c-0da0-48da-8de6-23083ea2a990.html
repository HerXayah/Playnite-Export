Description

Earthlingo is a vocabulary learning game. You play as an alien exploring Earth and trying to learn one of 14 different languages.

Every item in Earthlingo is an opportunity to learn a new word. The game world lets you associate each word with a location, improving recall.

Earthlingo teaches over 1000 nouns, adjectives and verbs, all for free! Learn Chinese, Danish, Dutch, English, Italian, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Russian, Spanish or Vietnamese.

Earthlingo is an ongoing project and is under full-time active development.
Current Game:
  • Learn vocabulary in 14 languages
  • 1,000 adjectives, nouns and verbs over 8 levels
  • Tests recognition of vocabulary through exploration

Development goals:
  • Late 2022 - Battle invading robots to practice spelling, 7 new languages
  • Early 2023 - Full story with cutscenes and character progression, 1500+ word count
  • Mid 2023 - 7 new languages, Voice recognition
  • Late 2023 - Quests for learning sentences

The best place to follow development, submit bug reports and feature requests is Earthlingo's Reddit page.

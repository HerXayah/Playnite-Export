Description

Derpy Dinos is a 2.5D side-scrolling arcade shooter. Smash, throw, and blast derpy dinosaurs for sport. The more carnage you produce, the more points you earn for a spot on the global leaderboards. Protect your ship from dino lasers and volcanic debris, collect power-ups, and earn achievements by completing challenging feats.

Use keyboard/mouse or an Xbox360 controller to play!

This is Last Days Motel.



A few survivors end up meeting here. Is it a new hope or just their final fate escaping from this cruel and relentless world? They will not give up and fighting against thier environment is the only option left!



Today is the end of the world, but also the first day of your survival.



By enjoying the first light of every new dawn and being optimistic, they begin to rebuild the motel, which is will be their only shelter in this unfriendly world as danger lurks around every corner.



Step into the deadly environments and scavenge for food, water, energy and supplies to keep you alive. Everything has value in a world like this, even a broken and rusty can found in the trash can be useful. Collect whatever you can get and build all kinds of facilities to reinforce your motel. With these facilities, you will be able to provide invaluable supplies like medicine, weapons and armor to your team members; train and treat them whenever necessary.



Again and again, you must brave the harsh expeditions searching for supplies, but be ready to encounter challenges from other survivors, whose target and goal is the same as yours.



Perhaps, they are willing to share.

Maybe, they are the only thing standing between you and those rare materials.

Or, maybe there is more than that…



Last Days Motel: where you simultaneously taste all the bitterness and sweetness from this wasteland. Come and join us in the fight!



Early Access Features:



The motel is your last shelter, build it however you like with different facilities to upgrade;
Level up your characters through reading books and various trainings;
Multiplayer PvP content – fight against other players in 5 different maps for supplies;
A random forest map available for solo PVE.

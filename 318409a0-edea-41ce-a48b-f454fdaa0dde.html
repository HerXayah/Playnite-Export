Description



For a long time now, you have secluded yourself within the island of Equinoxe. Your duty as its keeper is to maintain a careful balance of your world by using its main nourishing source of energy: water.

So far, you have been able to keep the lands’ harmony and never falter in your duty to protect it.

Lately, signs of an ancient and terrible calamity have started to appear, haunting you and threatening to break the sweet equilibrium you swore to defend.

Your objective is now to restore harmony and balance between two worlds reflecting each other. Journey through the path of unconditional acceptance of oneself while facing the opposing duality and complementarity to reach inner peace.

Guardian! You must do everything in your power to restore harmony and balance between those two worlds!



Water is the main energy source of the world of Equinoxe. Your mission as its guardian is to restore that source of energy and doing so, its balance. A challenging road awaits: solve puzzles that will stand in your way and explore the water paths.



You will have to rely on your logic and sense of perspective in order to progress in the world of Equinoxe. In cooperation with your other self, move around your environment with telekinesis and use perspective to rebuild your way to the next challenge.



Discover a rich and relaxing environment. Let yourself be carried away by a poetic universe and enjoy two mirrored worlds and their uniqueness.



  • Discover a unique gameplay, where you will be able to play between two worlds displaying simultaneously. Explore the worlds of the sun & the moon and interact between them to solve puzzles.

  • Solve enigmas that will challenge your logic senses.

  • Combine visual patterns using perspective to fuse or repair ruins and access to new areas.

  • Restore water in Equinoxe and retrieve the lost balance.

  • Find contemplation points and take a break to admire soothing and beautiful landscapes.

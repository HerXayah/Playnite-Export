Description

DIVEREAL: EXPLORE THE REAL WORLD WITH OTHERS


Play with or without the Oculus Rift

What if you could join your friends and explore places you never went before?

DiveReal is a social virtual world: people get in primarily to meet and talk to others. But unlike its classical counterparts, it does not rely on servers to put people together. Instead, DiveReal uses an overlay that grows as more people get in.
For the user, this means a different experience: the world is not anymore divided in "rooms", "islands" or "realms" but accepts everyone, possibly millions, in a unique seamless space.

EXPLORE

Take a tour in Paris, walk on the streets of New York or dive in Copacabana! On desktop or in VR, dive in the real world and find your favorite places.

MEET

Explore the real world and meet people from around the planet ! Make new friends and share your places.

SOCIAL

Dive in the real world, interact and share with divers.


DiveReal is in early development. Feel free to tell us what you think!

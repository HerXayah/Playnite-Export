Description

You play as Chickenman, a stranded weirdo in a chicken mech.

Your goal is to punch as many sheep as possible until the time runs out, every time you punch one you get some time back so make sure to move fast and never stop punching!


The game tracks high score and includes a single map.

Master Chickenman's mobility by jumping and dashing, and dispatch sheep with one mighty punch!

Sheep aren't hostile, but they need to leave, and since you're in the middle of nowhere, you have to punch them back home!

During the game, you have to fight the timer by punching sheep, every sheep gives you some time back, but time is always ticking so be fast and be vigilant!
If the timer hits 0 it's GAME OVER.

If you fall off the map it's also... GAME OVER.

Have fun and get hyped with Chickenman!!

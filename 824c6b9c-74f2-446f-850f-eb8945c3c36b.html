Description

Bouboum is a multiplayer bomber-hamster game!

Engage against other hamsters and more animals in explosive battles. Clear your way and your enemies in maze-like levels and be the last one to stay alive while the safe place is getting smaller!

Tactical Hamster on the rise

One of the keys to victory is not to rush things, but to observe and play mindfully! Gathering the power-ups and pick up items scattered around the map is a great way to secure victory. Get enhanced abilities by becoming a vampire or a zombie, or throw the classical banana peel to doom your enemies to a fateful end.

Evolve from hamsters to even cuter creatures

You can unlock not only as your typical good boy dogs and smarty cats, but also as cute pandas, deer, foxes and even dragons! You can do so by collecting Hazelnuts, tasty collectibles that drop from blown-up tiles during each game!

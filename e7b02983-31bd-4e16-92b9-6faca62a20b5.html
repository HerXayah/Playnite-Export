Description

Dungeon & Derision is a tactical turn-based game with some gameplay elements taken from the rogue-like genre !
Create your team to explore a mysterious island run by a powerful king !
Encounter random events during your exploration, your choice will be impactful for your heroes !
Permanent death with a system to upgrade definitly your heroes
Custom talent tree for each hero, upgrade them according to your play style

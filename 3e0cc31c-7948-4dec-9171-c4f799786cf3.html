Description

DMM Games’ PC browser game Dead or Alive Xtreme Venus Vacation, which was first announced at Tokyo Game Show 2016, will launch as a free-to-play title with item-based micro-transactions in Japan in 2017, the company announced.

The latest entry in the Dead or Alive Xtreme series, Dead or Alive Xtreme Venus Vacation features new and simple game systems, uses high-end 3D models, and has high-quality graphics by way of the new Soft Engine. Confirmed characters so far include Marie Rose (voiced by Mai Aizawa), Honoka (voiced by Ai Nonaka), and Hitomi (voiced by Yui Horie). There are also non-game modes such as viewing and photographing modes.

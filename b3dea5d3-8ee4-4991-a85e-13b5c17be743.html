Description

Explore a dreary motel for a glimpse into a mysterious story of childhood and family. Navigate rooms, pools, and lobbies to uncover more details to the unknown atmosphere and complete puzzles to delve farther into the life of a little boy and his family. Collect items, talk to NPCs, and travel between realms in an effort to unwind this strange and peculiar story. Under the direction of a mysteriously familiar figure waiting for him in his room, this boy must hunt down a childhood snack... but how will that help him out? From Open Alpha USC's third cohort, we give you Sinking Inn.

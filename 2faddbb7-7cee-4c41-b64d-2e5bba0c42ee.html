Description

Inspired by much older card games like magic the gathering, and Yugioh, World Leader Card Game (WLCG) seeks to engage the player, by giving them a set of cards that they need to utilize in order destroy their opponent's life bar. However, in those games, you would have some sort of mythic and/or fantastical creature/monster created by a writer; in this game, you take control of a real-world creature/monster, who in-fact used to be our governed leaders. WLCG offers 35 of the hundreds of 20th-century world leaders for the player to utilize to victory. Additionally, while playing the player will have the option to just hover their cursor over a card and get a brief bio on the person.

Note:
*WLCG & its creator, doesn't endorse any specific ideology or person.
**WLCG does not intend to disrespect or exonerate any of the individuals being used for entertainment purpose,
*** all bio's are meant to be kept brief and are not extensive enough to fully encapsulate one person

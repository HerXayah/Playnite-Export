Description

Where does the mind take you?

Red Bull Doodle Art is a global doodling competition where over 40.000 students worldwide are invited to share their creativity.

In its third edition, the best doodlers from around the world were invited to San Francisco where they could take doodling to the next dimension, literally. The resulting VR artworks were combined into the Red Bull Doodle Art - Global VR Gallery.

Download it and enjoy a rich multicultural environment and the possibility to see what some of the most creative students of the planet could come up with.

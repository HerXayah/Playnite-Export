Description

No game is fun with cheaters.

Easy™ eSports is the leading third-party anti-cheat client for Counter-Strike. We are unique by being pro-active, keeping focus on countering cheat mechanisms rather than on detecting them. This way you can have peace of mind that your opponents play clean, and won't be banned only days after having ruined your stats.

Easy™ eSports is used in competitive leagues and tournaments. The anti-cheat client is only used for joining those game servers that require it.

Supported games:

- Counter-Strike 1.6
- Counter-Strike: Condition Zero
- Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

To use Easy™ eSports:

- Install the anti-cheat client through Steam.
- Run the client.
- Start the game.

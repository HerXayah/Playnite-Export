Description

Formula Style Racing MMO.
Welcome to Phantom Racing – A Racing Game where you can compete with your Friends.
Create your own Racer and show the World, that you have what it takes to reach the Top.

Create your own Team, recruit new Drivers from all around the World and compete in Live Tournaments, Time Trials and much more. Join the Mini GP (limited) and compete for the fastest Race Times.



Social Features:
• Interact with your Friends
• Create your own Racing Team
• Use Emotes and chat with others
• Race together & against your Friends

Character-Design:
• Head
• Body
• Pants
• Shoes
• Gloves

Racing:
• Gamepad Support
• Wheel Support (upcoming)
• Gain Levels
• Compete on the very first Tracks

Tournaments:
• Global Scoreboard (Team & Player)
• Mini GP of the first published Tracks (Time Limited!)
• Live Race-Arena

Others:
• Language: English (more will follow)
• Download for Free, for people from all around the World

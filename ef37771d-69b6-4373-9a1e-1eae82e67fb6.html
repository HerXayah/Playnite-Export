Description

As you wake up inside your dream, you realize that you are missing a sock, so your ultimate goal is to find it!



Twitchoos RUN is a platform game where you are the hero, explore the environment, avoid traps, jump over bananas and carrots and collect gifts on your way.

Your viewers will either help you or trap you.
Count on them to trigger actions in the game via the comments.

Several game modes are possible:
  • Live Twitch (play with your viewers) or Offline (play with bots)
  • Speed Run (finish the game as fast as possible) or Gifts Run (grab all the gifts and stay alive)

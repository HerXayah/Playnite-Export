Description

Explore a New World and Create Your Own Destiny



Welcome to Polity, a social RPG in a user-generated world. Here, you can rule your own country, become a citizen of another player's country, and find a simple life as a farmer, forester, or whatever you want to be with many other professions. The choice is yours.





If you choose to rule your own country, you will be responsible for establishing your own laws and building your own cities. If you choose to become a citizen of another player's country, you will be able to contribute to its growth.

You can explore the world and discover its hidden treasures, or you can build a life that is truly your own. No matter what you choose to do in Polity, you will be part of a vibrant community of players from all over the world. Work together to build a better world, or compete for supremacy. The possibilities are endless.

The game takes place on a utopian planet called Blue Dot-2 (BD-2), a newly discovered planet in the Milky Way galaxy. It was discovered by an AI-powered spaceship, JP-01G.

The AI system called Snotra collected data about this mysterious planet: It is mostly covered with water with mysterious Mangrove Continents but there is no sign of life, or is it? Snotra who recently gained a kind of consciousness creates a new world for the memories and dreams of humankind.

This is where your journey begins. You will find your own path while exploring this mysterious planet, and you may even discover what happened to the old world of ours. Come and start to explore the world of Polity!

Explore a Mysterious World



  • BD-2 is a large and diverse ocean-based planet with only solid grounds on the Mangrove Continents and Islets placed by Snotra.
  • It is an ever-changing planet, so there is always something new to discover.

Create Your Own Destiny



  • In Polity, you are free to choose your own path.
  • You can be a farmer, forester, or anything else you want to be. With the growth of the countries, you can even create new professions. The possibilities are endless.

Work Together or Compete



  • Polity is a multiplayer game, so you can choose to work together with other players, compete against them, or simply socialize and build a community.
  • The choice is yours.

Build a Better World



  • Polity is a game about hope and possibilities.
  • You can help to build a better world for yourself and for future generations.

Achieve and Discover



  • In the pursuit of success, Polity invites you to complete various quests that are aligned with your profession.
  • Along the way, you can also discover the secrets of BD-2, the stories of the droids, and the history of a new civilization.

Become a Merchant



  • In-game trading is a fun way to acquire new items and resources.
  • You can buy and sell items from other players, or barter your own goods for theirs.

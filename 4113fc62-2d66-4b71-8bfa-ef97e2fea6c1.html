About This Game

The first part of the second episode of the Welcome To... Chichester trilogy, and details your first day on holiday in Chichester, England

The HD version is now more in line with what was originally hoped for when development originally started

The Story

Continuing from where WTC 1/Redux left off, the protagonist is unaware of his/her true location, thinking it's still somewhere in America. When faced with the truth, the protagonist has a hard time adjusting to the situation, requiring drastic help from the protagonist's nemesis and over-eager tourist guide. However, there may be more going on than meets the eye.

Features :

  • Updated to HD Resolution graphics
  • Grendel is now (at last) seen wielding her frying pan
  • 7 Endings over 6 routes, with one true ending
  • Play as a much put-upon male protagonist, or a continually harassed female protagonist, both featuring unique dialogue
  • Updated and extended story
  • Internal and Steam achievements - can you find them all ?
  • Mac version included

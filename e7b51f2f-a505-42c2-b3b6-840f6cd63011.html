BRINGING PUZZLES TO THE DIGITAL AGE


With more than 32 FREE puzzles included, Pixel Puzzles Ultimate has a large range of puzzles in various sizes for you to try out and enjoy for FREE.

EACH AND EVERY PUZZLE AND PIECE IS UNIQUE


Every puzzle cut is a one of a kind, in shapes no physical jigsaw could be.

Our jigsaws feature piece counts from 21 to all the way up to a huge 1410 pieces. Pixel Puzzles Ultimate brings you full size professional puzzles to the digital world.



With digital puzzles the possibilities are endless.

Multiple image orientations including square, rectangle and wide panorama images, in a massive variety of separately themed puzzle packs containing only the best images possible.

Browse and solve the types of puzzles you really want to build.



Participate every month in our FREE monthly challenge puzzle, You'll earn a special Ultimate Puzzle Pieces to use to spend in the Pixel Puzzles Ultimate KIOSK.

Earn Golden Puzzle Pieces by completing puzzles.

Use your Gold to unlock a wide range of bonus items including extra Jigsaw Puzzles, Themes, UI Customizations and much more. Like trading tickets for prizes at the old arcades.

IT'S A NEW WAY TO PUZZLE


Customize the game the way you want it, with a full range of options and settings to tweak the game as you see fit.

Puzzle your way!

Multiple themed play environments, all fully customizable for you to fine tune the jigsaw puzzling experience you’re looking for.

OTHER FEATURES:

  • Full range of sizes from 21 to 1410 pieces.
  • Beautiful puzzle images.
  • Auto saving, never lose your progress.
  • Peaceful puzzling atmosphere.
  • Traditional puzzle gameplay.
  • Wide range of customization options.
  • Multiple interactive play environments.
  • Power ups and hints to help solve puzzles.

Pixel Puzzles Ultimate, the definitive jigsaw puzzler for the PC platform.


No longer will a puzzler be left looking for that last piece, never again will the kitchen table be covered in tiny wooden shapes.

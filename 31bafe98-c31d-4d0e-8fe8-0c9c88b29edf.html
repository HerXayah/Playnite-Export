Description

CIDE-SCROLLING ONLINE SHOOTER


DEAD CIDE CLUB is a side-scrolling online battle game, the first in the Shooter and Battle Royale genres, where users engage in thrilling gunfights with other users from around the world against the backdrop of a concrete jungle.

Who will be #1? Battle Royale Solo mode open!


Die or survive alone! Compete with other players in fierce battles to be the last one standing. To survive, defeat strange monsters to acquire more powerful weapons and supplies.

Experience unique battles with a total of seven cartels!


There are cartels of nerds, prisoners, special agents, psychos, hipsters, etc. within DCC that have special weapons and perks. Unlock perks as you play and customize your battle experience. Infinite possibilities await!

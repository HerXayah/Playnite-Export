This is a free to play prologue for the full game Karagon!


Survive and shoot killer robots then reuse their parts to construct your own rideable robots!
Build a base, gather resources, craft weapons!
Shoot, construct, and ride robots in this post apocalyptic open world survival crafting free-to-play prologue!
Experience next gen cutting edge UE5 Lumen graphics technology!

  • Survive against robotic creatures using your forged guns and constructed rideable robots!
  • Shoot robots in the weakest spots to make them fall off and cause a lot of damage!
  • Craft & Forge guns, melee weapons, tools, machines, robot parts, and more!


  • Gather natural resources and machine parts from fallen robot parts and destroyed robots.
  • Construct killer rideable robots from the parts you collect and forge.
  • Build your base to store and protect your items, machines, and robots.


  • Scan & Defeat the most massive robots so you can construct your own!
  • Explore a post apocalyptic open world and choose your path to becoming the strongest!
  • Multiplayer: (full game only) join a community server or get your friends to survive the robots with you.
  • UE5 Lumen: experience next gen cutting edge UE5 lumen global illumination and reflections technology: this means the graphics will look better than ever before! (Since this was something we added very close to the Prologue's release, the current trailer and gifs don't showcase this, but all the screenshots (above) are taken with Lumen, and when you play you will have Lumen enabled by default.)

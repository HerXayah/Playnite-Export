"JUST SWING AND DUNK!"


Swing Dunk delivers a stunning combination of fast-paced basketball and a stylish hoverboarding experience. Ride your hoverboard to slip through your opponent’s defense and SWING the ball to shoot or throw down a SLAM DUNK. Train your skills and SWING your way to victory!

Basketball like no one has ever experienced!



  • It's not just point and shoot! Use the momentum from swinging the ball to shoot, pass, and DUNK!
  • 3 vs 3 full-court / 1 vs 1 half-court available! Never get bored!
  • Supports up to 8 players per game (4vs4), multiplayer
  • Supports game controller / keyboard + mouse

Your One and Only Character!



  • Various characters, gloves, appearance, hoverboard, and effects
  • Customize your very own character at the Locker
  • Plenty of equipable visual effects to make your best play shine even more!

Set perks to change your gameplay experience!



  • Set your own perks to create unique moves!
  • Choose the perfect perks and become the best teamplayer!

Description

Wild Runs is a 2D platformer enhanced with 3D graphics and stylised production, the game consists of 9 levels consecutively getting harder and harder to prove your mettle beat level 9 and prove you are the Wild Runner. Levels come from vast lush fields, snowy hilltops, to deep mines and dry rocky landscapes. All the adventure and fights to then finally prove yourself to the distress of the land, defeat it to claim your title as the Wild Runner.

Description

Welcome to the dark world of the Neon Knight:

Neon Knight is a 2D action side-scroller developed for Windows PC. It is the epic tale of a supernatural resurrected warrior who travels throughout the dystopian kingdom intending to end the current reign of terror and find his family. It is a fast-paced action game with a specific 2D monochrome graphic style and dark dystopian atmosphere. It partly draws inspiration from the classic 2D games from the past, but also adding modern gameplay elements and compelling story.


FEATURES:

*Fast-paced action
*Story full of reversals
*Interesting locations and characters
*Specific 2D monochrome graphic style
*Rich singleplayer campaign
*Additional Survive mode

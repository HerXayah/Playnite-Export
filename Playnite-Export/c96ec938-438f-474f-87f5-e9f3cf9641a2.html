Description

It's not violence if they're vermin.
Perfect Vermin is 10-15 minute first person narrative exploration game featuring timer based puzzle elements and multiple levels.

Your task is simple, exterminate all vermin. The sledge hammer you have been provided is capable of destroying almost all office appliances and furniture that may be uncooperative. Your time is running out.

Perfect Vermin was developed by Talia bob Mair and Angad Matharoo. With Sound Design by Chris Dang and Composed by Daniel Shypilov.

Description

Welcome to Egypt ! With your friends, play as gods in training and complete magical recipes for your classes ! Have fun : cheat, cast spells… But be careful, Râ your teacher will gladly scold you if you get caught !

About:

Tiny Gods of the Nile is a local co-op game for 2 to 4 players where your goal is to make a potion in a limited amount of time, watched by a strict teacher, who will judge and score your potion !

Student Project

Inspired by Overcooked, this game was created by second year students at IAD, with the goal to develop a co-op game. The music and sounds were made by sound design students at Audio Workshop.

2 to 4 players

During the game, you will be able to team up as a group of maximum 4 players to find the right ingredients and successfully complete the recipe as fast as possible. You will be able to carry each other and throw items at each other, transform them and work as pairs to open sarcophages, but nothing stops you from playing tricks on your friends.

The levels

Râ will give you a grade depending on the quality of your work. This grade will allow you and your friends to unlock new levels with more ingredients, more challenging recipes and hidden items… Cheating will become more and more needed ! Be wary of Râ!

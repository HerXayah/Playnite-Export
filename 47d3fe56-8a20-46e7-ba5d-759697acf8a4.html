Description

In Pizza Apocalypse, you take control of 3 daring delivery people armed with a chef certified arsenal of deadly weapons and gadgets.

Delivering Pizza in the mutant monster apocalypse can be tough, your troops must work together to survive and combo the many abilities at their disposal for tremendous results!

But be wary, for the mutants possess powerful abilities and unique combos of their own. In order to ensure every delivery arrives in tip-top condition your troops must guard each other from incoming attacks whilst also picking the enemies off and strategising which enemies pose the greatest threat to you...

With a unique, colourful, and sometimes gruesome art style by Louis Morel, Pizza Apocalypse gives an insight into a crazy post-apocalyptic world filled with interesting characters who wish only to enjoy the sweet sweet taste of cheesy dough from a world before it came to an end, happy delivering!

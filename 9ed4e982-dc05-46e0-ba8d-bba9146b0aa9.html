Description



Choose your gear wisely and make it from shelter to shelter. Your mood plays an important role in your survival. Pick up airdrops or cut down trees to get loot. Be careful of traps, rough weather conditions and wild animals that attack you. Hike on tons of procedural generated "real-world GPX" or fantasy trails.

Competition

Enjoy playing against computer or human controlled hikers.

Unlock Inventory

Give your rucksack a new style with badges and make your character unique with a variety of hats.

Level Editor

Create unique trails yourself or import GPX files.

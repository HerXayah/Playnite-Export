Description

It's Bombin' Time!
Climb into the pilot seat! Team up, shoot down your opponents, collect their bombs, then bomb their ships to smithereens! The first team to sink all enemy ships wins the round. brainCloud Bombers supports 8 player simultaneous matches with empty slots filled in by bots! It's time to get bombin' !

As Bombers supports multiple platforms (including web), you will be prompted to create a quick (& free) account. This allows you to login and play across all supported platforms. The game is simple, but fun - we hope you like it!

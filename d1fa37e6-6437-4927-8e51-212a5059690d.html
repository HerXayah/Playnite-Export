Description



Space Probes is a Shoot Em Up (SHMUP) inspired by productions from the 90s released on SNES, MEGADRIVE, NEO-GEO, PC-ENGINE etc.
You control a small ship and can get more than 20 different skills to help you progress through the levels. Dynamic and demanding, will you be able to take up the challenge?

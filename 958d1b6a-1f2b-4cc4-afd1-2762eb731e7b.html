Description

Ghost Hunter is a competitive party game. You can play it with your local friends and online friends.

Background

The story takes place in a world of science and technology and supernatural, since the invention of the the SpiritGun, the ghost hunter has become a rise of the profession, the annual ghost of the hunter contest attracted heroes from all over the world to participate, and you are one of them.

How to play

Use SpiritGun to absorb ghost and then shoot them to the cross. It sounds very simple, isn't it?
But it is hard to win the ghost(AI) and the adversary in the limited time.

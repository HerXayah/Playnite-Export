Description

- The Sand Dunes -

The Sand Dunes is an action game with shooter and bullet hell elements. You will have to complete levels exploring and defeating multiple enemies.

Delve into Diana's last mission.
Liberate your brother Amit and the other Gods from their destruction and vengeance desires.

  • Explore the dunes. Find secrets and upgrades (new spells, or stats boosts).

  • Complete different challenges in order to unlock secret zones or to continue the adventure.

  • Defeat multiple bosses and survive chaotic battles using your spells, dashes and magic shields.

Description

Idle Wasteland is an Incremental Idle RPG in a post-apocalyptic world.

You stand alone in the wasteland and must fight monsters, salvage resources and refine materials to survive!

Key features:


- Defeat monsters, gain power, unlock new zones and hunt down bounties!

- Gather a large variety of loot and discover rare salvaging spawns!

- Use resources gathered to refine materials, craft permanent power-ups and suit upgrades, enhance item efficiency and more!

- Unlock and cast fighting and utility spells, discover unique essence to enhance your spells!

- Hunt 20+ mutant creatures that appear during gameplay, each with their own loot table!

- Earn and level up companions to unlock powerful bonuses!

- Complete various Tasks and Missions to unlock stat enhancements!

- Find and use items to make yourself more powerful and provide helpful utilities!

- Idle Wasteland has a plethora of content, play the game to discover much more!

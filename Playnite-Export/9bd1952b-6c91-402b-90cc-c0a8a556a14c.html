Description

Social Deduction & Battle Royale


  • One player starts as the illusionist and tries to kill everyone.
  • Others try to identify the illusionist.
  • If the illusionist dies, they leave an orb behind.
  • When a player takes the orb, they become the illusionist.

Skills suited for assasination...

...and mind games.

Positional Voice Chat

You can only speak with players near you. Dead players can't speak.

No In-Game Purchases. No Progression.

It's a simple game. Pick a name, customize your character, and join or create a lobby.

Features

  • Top down camera
  • Right-click movement
  • Skills with cooldowns
  • Can unlock skills by collecting stars
  • Fast paced gameplay thanks to short rounds
  • Chat box communication for those not preferring voice

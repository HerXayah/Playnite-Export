Description

Dungeon Brawlers is an epic PvP battle game where you battle your friends for glory! Pick a character each with a unique ability and scavenge the map looking for weapons to turn against your opponents. Battle it out with a variety of weapons including Swords, Hammers, Lasers, and Spears. Be on the look out for the boon of the Dungeon: The Power of the Cyclops! Destroy the Cyclops' Eye to gain it's power and deliver a devastating blow to your opponents.

Battle with up to 4-players or duke it out with the AI. Whoever can defeat their opponents and get the required score the quickest will be declared the new King of the Dungeon!

Description

Fadeout: Undergroundis a Third Person shooter in the vein of classic PC games from the late 90s and early 2000s. Inspired by games like Quake, Unreal Tournament, and Team Fortress with some unique twists.

Fast-Paced Combat

Each playable character features unique combat traits that never force you into playing a specific role.
Pick up weapons & gadgets on the map to augment your play style.
Fight in online multiplayer with several classic game-modes including; Death Match, Team Death Match, Capture the Flag, Arena, Team Arena, & Conquest!

Features

4 Maps
6 Gamemodes
+1 Bonus Mutation Mode (Randomizer)
22 Weapons
8 Playable characters

Description

DogeFight is an EPIC Third-Person Multiplayer Fighting game 👍
Play with the most incredible meme characters of all times like Stonks or Doge in a competitive survival game mode.



More precisely, this game consists in spawning on a map with up to 6 other players, and each player has 3 lifes. You have to be the last player alive by fighting your opponents until everybody lost their life's, and cannot respawn anymore.
By winning a game, you will collect a big amount of social credit, which allows you to compare your level with the DogeFight community.
You can also gain social credit by collecting the Amogus coins all around the different maps.

This game is designed to play with friends in the same lobby, but it's also possible to play with randoms thanks to the server list.

But be careful not to be SUS.

Features

  • Up to 6 Players
  • 8 Different Characters
  • 6 Maps

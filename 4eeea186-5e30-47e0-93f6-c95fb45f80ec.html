Description

Here it is! The short-awaited sequel to The Battles of Spwak 2!

Trapped in another dimension, this time willingly, help Spwak overcome the Bosses of each Map and defeat the Homeless Guy again. Evade Enemies to get Flames, spend your Stat Points and learn new Skills.

Advance at your own pace. Battle everyone, outmaneuver Enemies or do both. Invest in HP or MP. In Battle, use Fast or Normal Speed, Pause at will or Retry whenever you want.

Features

  • Simple, fast-paced gameplay
  • Player Character battles automatically - switch between Skills
  • 105 Unique Battles, 7 Bosses
  • Enemies chase you when you get too close
  • 98 Flames to get - Can you get them all?
  • Up to 196 Stat Points
  • 7 Different Maps
  • Up to 8 Usable Skills
  • Redistribute freely your Player Character Stats'
  • Enemy Stats are available in the Map
  • 100% accurate Skills
  • 10 Achievements

Controls

Map
  • Space / Enter / Z / Left Click: Proceed
  • X, Esc, Right Click: Cancel / Skip
  • Arrow Keys, Left Click: Movement
  • T: Go To Title Screen Prompt
  • A: Go To Audio Settings
  • S: Go To Save Menu
  • L: Go To Load Menu
  • H: Go To Help Section
  • M: Go To Skill Menu
  • -, - (Numpad): Lowers HP by 100 (if possible)
  • =, + (Numpad): Raises HP by 100 (if possible)
  • Page Down: Lowers MP by 10 (if possible)
  • Page Up: Raises MP by 10 (if possible)

Battle
  • Space: Pause / Resume
  • Shift: Fast Speed / Normal Speed
  • R: Retry Battle
  • 1: Select Normal Attack
  • 2: Select Heal
  • 3: Select Burn (if available)
  • 4: Select Sacrificial Attack (if available)
  • 5: Select Reflect (if available)
  • 6: Select Softening (if available)
  • 7: Select Sacrificial Power (if available)
  • 8: Select Drain (if available)

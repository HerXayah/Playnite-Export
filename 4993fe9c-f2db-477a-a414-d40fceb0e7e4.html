Description

In this open world RPG Rogue-Like choose your own adventure you take control of Dre, an 18 year old drug dealer with nothing to lose. You start off with 4 different character classes to choose from that all have different positive and negative attributes. After which you are set free with-in the city of Cloverland to decide how to build your empire. Start a gang, a drug distributing network, a night club or even a strip club but each decision coming with it's up and downs. This game is still early access but is fully playable with new content on the horizon. We hope to see you in Cloverland. Trap on!

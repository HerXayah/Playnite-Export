Description

Frick Frack is a puzzle simulation game about hurting what you love the most. You play as a miner digging wells for a fracking company. You must find the perfect work-life balance but the work you do directly affects the world around you. Good luck.

Frack
- Dig tunnels, extract oil, get money

Spend
- Use your fracking money to buy essentials, or more

Repeat.

Description

《Ganghood Survival》is a game project transformed from mobile to PC. Currently, not all the content in mobile version is quite suitable for the PC one. However, we have planned to gradually improve the art quality to a higher level in PC version. Moreover, the playing methods and content will be constantly increased.

《Ganghood Survival》 is a hardcore competitive action game based on Chinese Wushu theme. At the beginning, dozens of players will be randomly distributed at anywhere in the map, and play as a ‘Knight-errant’ with nothing in every game.

Developing Approach:
At the beginning, players will enter the map by riding a huge eagle owl with nothing. They need to strengthen themselves by collecting resources and learning wushu books. As time goes by, players get less and less safe area in the map. Eventually, only one player could win the game and learn the “Wushu of Wuwang” in order to beat the monster. However, the great winner also sacrifices himself for saving human and the world.

Game features:
1. Highly free playing method: players can freely use several types of weapons, wushu, close combat, long-distance attack, and qing kung (fly).
2. Exciting and fresh game experience: random distribution at anywhere in the map, gives fresh sense to players in every game.
3. Real wushu action in game: professional wushu team, almost all the wushu actions are cached by Optical motion capture system.

Fang Liu, Professional Film and Television Director, Representative Art: 《Kung Fu Warlords 2017》 and《Handsome Siblings》. He is the chief action designer of our game 《Ganghood survival》, leading his processional wushu team and design the excellent wushu action for this game.

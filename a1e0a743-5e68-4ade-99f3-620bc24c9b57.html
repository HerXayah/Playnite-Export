Description

Caveblazers Together is an online multiplayer mod for Caveblazers.

It allows to play the game over the internet with up to 4 players at once.

The mod uses Steam for connectivity and invitations - no need to open ports.

It supports vast majority of base game's features as well as some specific to it - such as chat and chat commands.

Please keep in mind that this isn't something that the game have been developed with so you may encounter minor oddities.

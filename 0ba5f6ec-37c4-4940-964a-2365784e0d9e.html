Description

A real time and fast paced tactical battle game in a world of wizards, warriors and monsters. Hand drawn creatures and soldiers fight it out over a campaign, custom scripted maps and a single player skirmish mode.

Wage warfare against undead hordes. Create your own custom scripted maps and campaigns. Defeat your enemies!

Features Include

Single Player Campaign
A series of missions as you take Grumthorr through battles against an evil invader.
Custom Scripted Maps
A collection of custom trigger based scripted maps of varying gameplay
Skirmish Mode
A simple quick gameplay mode that plays a little like capture the flag
Level Editor
A level editor for creating your own campaigns, scripted maps, with full access to the trigger system.
Moddable
Users can create their own units, graphics, sounds, triggers and even gui elements with easy access to the game's textual data files and .png and .wav graphics.
Fun!
Of course - what game would be complete without being fun to play!

My hope is that you enjoy playing the game as much as I have enjoyed creating and playing it myself.

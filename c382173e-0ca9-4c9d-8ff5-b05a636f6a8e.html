Description

Dive into a World of Limitless Imagination with the ST World!

ST World is the next-generation game engine specializing in voxel world-building and AI-assisted creation. With advanced algorithm design and state-of-the-art machine learning capabilities, creative input in the 3D world is more accessible than ever.

Unleash Your Creativity with Game-Changing Tools

Our Console AI and Material AI tools transform the creative process, making world-building more accessible and effortless. Create realistic environments, tweak the weather, and modify the time of the day, all with natural language input.


Experience the Power of Effortless Open World Creation

At the core of ST World is our innovative sparse voxel engine, providing high-resolution voxel rendering and efficient performance across devices. It empowers you to bring your visions to life without complex modding or extensive development knowledge.

Join a Thriving Community of Creative Visionaries

Connect with fellow creators in the upcoming asset and map marketplace, sharing your masterpieces and discovering unique creations from others. We foster a supportive community that inspires and helps each other grow.


Your Journey Starts Here

Become a part of the ST World community, and let's build worlds together! Wishlist and embark on the adventure that awaits as you unlock the full potential of your imagination.

ST Engine: Current Features

  • Cutting-edge sparse-voxel level editor with 16 times more cubic resolution than Minecraft.
  • Brush, Volume, Freeform, and Smoother tools, enabling rapid creation and editing of intricate, high-resolution voxel environments.
  • A vibrant map community to find, download, share, and showcase your creations and games.
  • Material AI features unlimited block creation with natural language input, sparking endless creativity and innovation.
  • Console AI features allow you to create natural environments and change sky weather and time of the day with natural language input.

Anticipated Future Features

  • Voxel Terminal: An extension of the full engine that quickly import and export creation from and to Minecraft and Schematics mods.
  • AI Upscaler: Import your low-resolution Minecraft world, filling details with smaller blocks with AI.
  • Riftwalkers Storyline and Open World gameplay: A dual-era adventure with rich exploration between the ancient and modern world.
  • Liquid and Transparent Material: Better glasses, liquid simulation, and shading.
  • Weapon and Enemy: Moddable combat system, with several kinds of enemy and spawning options.
  • Multiplayer: Collaborative building and gameplay with the multiplayer server.
  • Character Customization: more your look, clothes, and equipment selection.

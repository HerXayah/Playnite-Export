The insanely popular Flash game DOTS got a huge sequel!

Gobble up Dots to fill your Rainbow Meter, then destroy your enemies with RAINBOW POWER!
In NewGame+ upgrades are taken away and replaced with Punishments! The bigger the punishment you choose, the more points you get per Dot!

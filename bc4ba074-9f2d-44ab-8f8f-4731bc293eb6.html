Description

After a vehicle accident, a man is forced to check a nearby gas station for help, unaware of its ghastly resident.
FPS Indie Horror where you use your phone's flashlight to help navigate the property, look for a key and interacting with the environment while trying to find a way to get back on the road.

Just a classic low-quality indie horror game for a throwback to the earlier days of cheap games. Think of this as playing out a short creepypasta.

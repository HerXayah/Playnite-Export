Description



Block Arena is an exciting multiplayer game where you will be spawned into a map made entirely of blocks with a group of competitors. With each step you take the block will fall below you leaving you with less and less of the map left to traverse. As the map gets increasingly smaller you will be forced to make daring jumps and to use your abilities (super jump, dash, grappling) strategically in an attempt to secure your place as the last player standing!



Block Arena features a fun ranking system that ranges from Bronze 1 to Gold 3 and is automatically adjusted based on the players win rate and performance!



Multiple maps are featured and will be constantly added to allow for fun and fresh gameplay.

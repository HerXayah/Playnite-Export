[1. Introduction to Games]
Three Kingdoms of China is a free, open-source single-machine strategy three-country game developed by the domestic folk team. The game does not have any commercial nature, hoping to create a dream of Three Kingdoms for the fans of the three kingdoms.
The computer version of Three Kingdoms of China has been developing silently for nearly 10 years. The original avatar, military generals, game UI interface, program optimization and new maps are in progress. Looking forward to all kinds of operations, procedures, art, modeling, UI and other Internet elites and historical figures joining the project, making their own free open source games!
The development of the game only depends on the majority of enthusiastic players to propagate themselves, and hope that you can support the domestic, promote Chinese culture!
Three Kingdoms of China - Three Kingdoms of Chinese People!
Three Kingdoms of China - Our Three Kingdoms of China!


[2. Game Characteristics]
1. Maps are very close to history.
Supermaps are precisely surveyed and mapped according to historical terrain. All terrain, rivers and city pools are distributed with strict respect for historical facts, and their strategic position is very prominent. In the Northern Expedition, Guandu, Jieting, Yinping, the lack of food in Nanman, the difficulty in the Northwest desert, the surrounding mountains of Binzhou, all directions of Jingzhou, and the river-crossing of Yangzhou were governed.

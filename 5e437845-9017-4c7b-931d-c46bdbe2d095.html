Description

Batterneers is an exciting, action-packed game for up to 4 players. Fight across a number of different maps and game modes using a range of abilities and powerups.

Do note that as of now, Batterneers only supports online and LAN multiplayer. Local multiplayer is currently not available.



Battle Across a Range of Different Game Modes

  • Deathmatch - Fight to be the last one standing with the highest score in this classic game mode.
  • Gem Chase - Hold onto the precious gem for as long as you can while avoiding your fellow players.
  • Treasure Hunt - Collect the most treasure to win the game! But watch out, as other players can try to sabotage you.



Choose Your Approach to Combat

  • Cannons - In order to be the best, you'll need to learn how to fire your cannons, calculate the trajectory and use a range of unique powerups to your advantage. These include tracking balls, boomerangs, summoning the kraken, sun beams, explosives and more!
  • Bombs - The most dangerous weapon in Batterneers. Bombs can be picked up and thrown at pursuing enemies, but watch out as just one rogue cannon ball can make everything go wrong.
  • Ship - Using the right powerups, your ship can be as deadly as any weapon in the game. Spikes, ocean swirls and rapid reloading will make anyone run away in fear.

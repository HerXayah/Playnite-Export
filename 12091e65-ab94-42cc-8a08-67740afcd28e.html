Description

Have you ever wanted to raise an army of the dead and wipe out pesky humans?
Is the tedious management of the army's infrastructure something you look forward to?
Can you control your army numbers and deal with vampire and zombie numbers swelling without your leave?

12 undead types to raise.
20 buildings.
4 raw materials.
8 resources.

Take a walk on the indie dark side.

This is the Tycoon-style Necromancer simulator.
Build up your base, collect corpses, convert them into minions.
Allocate jobs to your undead host. More jobs open-up as the game progresses.
Defend against human attacks and assault them back.
Research improvements for the infrastructure.
Take over all human villages to win the game.

Description

"Dr. Darkglass" wants to Conquer the World, so he stole all the Energy supplies to create his Evil Robots. There is only one benign and that is "Elec Dude", he is the last Hope to save Mankind.

This Game is about collecting all the Energy per Level and there by getting to the next Level. But be careful not to collide with Enemy Robots.

- Pixel Art
- Chiptune Sound
- Various Enemy
- Fun Gameplay
- 20 Level

Perfect for a Speedrun!!!

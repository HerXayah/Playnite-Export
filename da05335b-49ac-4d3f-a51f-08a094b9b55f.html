Description

A Trippy Physics Based RPG. Make your way around a maze and try to figure out what is happening between different NPCs as they tell you things based on an Eastern Philisophy known as Ling. This game features strobing colors and lights and those sensitive to epileptic seizures should not play this game.



Included in Package: Basic HUB Levels of Soul Symphony, NPCS, and dialog. Content Randomizes each time the game is started or you go through portals. Talk to all the NPCs and learn the story "in-between".

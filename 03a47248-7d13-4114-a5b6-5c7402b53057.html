Description

Gear up! Obtain secret items or master a skill - and join thousands of other players in this massive 2D adventure!

Etherion Online RPG is a modern-2D action MMORPG which takes place on a newly inhabited planet called Etherion which features scarce resources and a beautiful agrestic architecture. Purchase a weapon, level it up by using it on monsters or other players, and prestige it for rewards!

Capture bases with a clan or create your own and attempt to take over the land of Etherion! If you're more of a lone-wolf player - venture off and defeat bosses for extremely rare items or become the solidified as the best duelist in the game at Eden Spar. All weapons in Etherion feature attachments, levels, and reward skins for prestiging - which makes every gun unique in its own way. Complete the unique battle pass which releases every 90 days with exclusive, themed, and limited content! Create your own personal character with our custom character creation system features customizable heads, eyebrows, mouths, eyes, beards, and more!

Create your Character
Etherion Online RPG features a FREE customization system which utilizes tons of different assets for hair, eyebrows, eyes, beards, mouths, and a variety of colors. There are currently over a million different combinations. If you want to create your own CUSTOM outfit then purchase an upload ticket from the store and find the outfit templates on our Discord!

Skills
Etherion currently features skills such as weaponry, health, mining, and smithing - but we are working on woodcutting, fishing, firemaking, cooking, bounty hunting, crafting, and much more! Skills also feature a secret pet for each skill - they are extremely rare!

Open-World PvP & PvM
On Etherion you can battle against entire clans, individual players in a spar match, or go defeat rare bosses for their super-rare items / weapons and trophies. You choose your own path!

Collecting
In the world of Etherion, there are many different secret items, rewards, and collectibles. Sometimes you’ll have to find them, You can dig for them, or get lucky by defeating something and have them rewarded! From secret pets to weapons, to expensive items and trophies, there’s a lot in store for those who enjoy collecting novelty items.

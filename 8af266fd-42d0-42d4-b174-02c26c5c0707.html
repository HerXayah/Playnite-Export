Description

As a special unit deployed by Astral Cortex Industries, you are tasked with restarting the moon's infrastructure by finding and reactivating the monoliths across its surface. Manage your suit's energy to ensure your survival, find and trade modules to help you traverse the dangerous terrain and deal with enemy droids, malfunctioning due to the corrupting influence of something below the surface...

Features:

  • Survival, traversal and action gameplay.
  • A moody and atmospheric lunar setting.
  • A spherical level with a map and inventory system.
  • Delivery missions to earn extra credits for upgrades and equipment.
  • A global leaderboard entry of your final score, based on completed objectives and gameplay stats.

Party Maker Farm Fest is a simulator of an event agency that organizes events in amazing places. Birthdays, weddings, bachelor parties or Halloween fun in an extraordinary space. Unleash your creativity, get the right equipment, prepare a place, use what you find.


In the Party Maker Farm Fest, you will have the opportunity to organize the summer fair. However, you will also have to prove that you can handle it.


Clear the level of rubbish to pimp the party spot.


Lots of amazing decorations to choose from.


To get a REAL blast, you might want to design your own firework show!


Discover additional activities and unique quests on each level. If you like puzzles or are passionate about classical and electronic music or even motorization you will find lots of fun.

Here are the most important tips of a crazy party maker:

  • Complete various orders of your clients. Take care of the budget and clients' satisfaction.
  • By making parties for others, you collect party coins to unlock special decorations.
  • Find collectable items to unlock new office decor.
  • Various types of tools will help you in decorating and renovating.
  • Sometimes you will have to deal with unexpected events and also meet extraordinary characters.
  • Some decorations you must create from the junk you find.

Thanks to the large number of decorations, Party Maker gives you the opportunity to create the party of your dreams, arranging unusual places, creating unique atmosphere, and, above all, it can be an inspiration for your own real-life Party.

VTuber Maker is the #1 virtual avatar live broadcast software focusing on VTuber and VUP services, also its a professional virtual presentation application which can video presentation, photo presentation and so on. All of these powered by Live3D.

Whether worry about no one follow your live stream?
Whether your channel exposure not too much?
Whether is difficult to do a presentation while live?
It doesn't matter, Live3D is a professional to help you solve such problems.

VTuber Maker, makes your avatar live broadcasts and virtual presentation stand out like professionals - even if you are new to VTuber or Video Creators.

VTuber Maker, a virtual youtuber software, What can be done?

We provide a lot of interesting functions for VTuber, you can try and use this VTuber software, and interact with the audience and fans online. And in conjunction with the VTuber Editor, more creativity can be exerted.


Live3D will focus on the global virtual sandbox platform in the future, integrating content creation and entertainment, so stay tuned!

If you want to communicate with more like-minded VTuber, welcome to join our Discord community to share your favorite Virtual/VTubing culture.

【VTuber Maker】Current Core Functions:

1.High-precision Facial Capture
  • For VTuber, only one camera is needed, and you can accurately Facial Capture
  • High-performance, high-sensitivity facial capture technology, even in bright or dim environments
2.Audio based lipsync
  • What to do if you don’t have a webcam, don’t worry, we also provide professional voice tracking, which can also achieve the perfect anthropomorphic effect
3.Professional 3D avatars software, and supports custom models
  • A large number of built-in 3d high-quality models for you to avatar live broadcast quickly, and support for uploading VRM models
  • All models support fast editing of character painting, accessories, and special effects
4.New avatar live broadcast method: window capture
  • Support fast window capture, such as games, browsers, music players and other windows
  • Replace the obs push method of traditional VTuber software: transparent push/green screen push avatar + game/content window capture combined into a live screen,resulting in loss of avatar effect
5.Comprehensive upgrade of virtual presentation effect
  • Character effect switching, multiple people acting on the same stage, character frame presentation, character switching between different pose idles (standing, sitting, raising legs, sitting on the ground), etc
6.Document/Video Presentation
  • By importing powerpoint, documents, pictures, videos, you can make your live broadcast or creative content richer
7.Virtual webcam
  • Through the virtual webcam, you can quickly perform virtual presentation on Zoom, Discord, Skype, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams and other software.
  • You can also directly push the virtual screen on obs, and cooperate with our Presentation System to become a presentation expert
8.Shortcut action panel, and blockly custom action, expression programming module
  • Built-in 6 popular shortcut actions, allowing you to quickly trigger human animation effects
  • Different from the traditional 3D animation software, we have introduced a new blockly programming action, which realizes more advanced trigger methods
    such as idle action switching, shortcut key trigger action, mouse trigger action, etc
Of course, we also support more other functions, welcome everyone to experience and understand
For example: transparent streaming (OBS, Streamlabs, etc), Pop-up window mode, 3d animation video, video background, custom layer (background, overlay layer, custom upload), full screen switching, leap gesture motion capture, etc.

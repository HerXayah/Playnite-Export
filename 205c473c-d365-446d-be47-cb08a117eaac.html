Description



The evil enemy stole a friend of our hero, sent unprecedented woes to the planet, moreover, he imprisoned the main character in the labyrinths of monochrome hell.
The protagonist is a cat. To whom nothing remains but to arm himself in the fight against the army of devilish creatures and become a HellCat.

Go on a quest for truth into the depths of purgatory, along with an armed to the teeth cat in a pretty red scarf.
Pixel top-down shooter with an explosive retro-wave soundtrack!

Description

Embody Margo, an almost fairytale like princess.

Housed in her room at the top of a tower, she faces an abundant amount of suitors who show up to court her. Until then, a grandma dragon was protecting the tower but as she retire the tower is defenceless. The princess will have to kick out suitors by herself.

Create your very own tower, connect castle rooms, place traps, sneak in galleries, unlock new traps, request your allies, and send suitors packing before they reach your bedroom!

Epic Tower for Tireless Lovers has two distinct phases : a thinking and building phase and an action phase.
During the first phase, you'll arrange your castle to create a maze so suitors get lost and you'll place traps to snatch intruders.
The second phase allows you to move in the castle you made to eliminate suitors who are roaming in your tower. You'll have to activate and repair traps, call out your allies while keeping an eye on your bedroom door to avoid suitors to go in.

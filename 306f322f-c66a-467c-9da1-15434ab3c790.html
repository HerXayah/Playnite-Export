Description

Shoot 'em up with physics

A single mine detonation in this bullet hell game will send you flying around the screen. But never fear! Though the enemies keep coming faster, you have a teleportation module and an arsenal of weapons to defeat them. Blast them with your machine gun, melt them with your laser and smack them silly with your shotgun.

Fair warning: this game has a moderate learning curve.

Play Many Game Modes

  • Survival - Play against endless waves of enemies.
  • Campaign - Defeat a carefully choreographed onslaught of enemies.
  • Thermal Meltdown - Avoiding getting melted by the cosmic power.
  • Color Defender - Pick up the right colored weapon to defeat the snakes.
  • Dodge This - How long can you survive in space as it gets more crowded?
  • There could be more in the future if I see significant player interest.

Other Key Features

  • Compare your score against the top 10 other players.
  • Watch replays of your games or from the leaderboards. You can start mid-game to practice!
  • Earn achievements when you've reached certain goals.

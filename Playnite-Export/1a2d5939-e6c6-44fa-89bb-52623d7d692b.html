Description

Features: sky mirror, water, salt flats, dreamland, wonderland, fresh, dreamy, abstract, relaxing, puzzle, reflection, flying, gliding, physics, left right brain, positive energy.

Scenes:



Mirror of Introspection: You will play a puzzle solving game. You need to get positive energy, avoid obstacles, solve the puzzle and find the exit of the maze within a limited time.




Mirror of Illusion: You will play a left right brain training game. Your reflected image in the mirror is opposite to you. When you turn left, your reflected image will turn right. When your virtual image touches negative energy, you will lose positive energy as well. You need to go through the obstacles and find the exit within a limited time.




Mirror of Freedom: You will play a flying game. Facing a dynamic environment, you have to collect batteries to maintain the flying power, overcome the effect of airflow and avoid obstacles at the same time.

Description

'신기록 : A Compendium of Ghosts' is a role-playing game based on the webtoon of the same name.

Sang returns home as a fully fledged hunter. On his way, he gets caught up in a very strange event. You have to accompany him on his journey to gather clues and puzzle out what is going on.

Features
-Every character has his or her own story. Strike up a friendly conversation and you might get a glimpse of their life or earn a special reward.
-It’s a Pixel art role-playing adventure game. Feel the nostalgic retro vibe only a pixel art game can provide!
-Be curious and keep talking to the characters. They may drop some clues they didn’t provide the first time. Find out what really happened to Sang!
-Sang’s village has delicious snacks hidden in clay jars on the side of the road. How generous of them!
-You might be able to find the creators hiding somewhere within the game
-Find the characters from the original cartoon making a cameo appearance

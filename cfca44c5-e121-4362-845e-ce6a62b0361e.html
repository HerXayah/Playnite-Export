Description

3D Ray Tracing Block Construction Builder Free Video Game, supporting users generated free and paid items for sale.
Block building game, in which the world construction system is a very similar to Minecraft creating regime, using 346 unlimited customizing materials and 32 basic shapes in two game modes- 1st person builder and 1st person explorer/walker.
Use Ray Tracing technology to create as beautiful as you can imagine dynamic worlds, scenes, levels, items.
Share [free] or sell [paid] your items in the Steam Workshop.

Which is the most beautiful scene you can possible create, limited only by your hardware- the mind and the computer?

Luxocraft is an updated and modified version of the original Celestial Creator (released on 17.07.2017) game, but with some changed, new features and most important - support of the Ray Tracing Technology - "The Magic Rays" or "The Gods Rays".
Almost all materials have visual identity (thumbnails) and names to the UE4 Editor materials for easy recognizing from UE4 users community.
However, if you change only one from many customization parameters with one or two clicks, you can create completely new, unique and custom material and looks.
Grow your creativity and innovations with one click.

Description


Pre-Shave is a narrative shaving simulator game.

You play as Saam preparing to get ready for a trip. Explore the anxieties of his nervous mind, thwart your excessive body hair with the use of your powerful shaving gear, and don't forget to brush your teeth!



Features
  • Wacky physics with unique controls
  • "Realistic" shaving mechanics
  • Multiple gadgets to groom and clean with
  • Lots of body and facial hair for you to sculpt / personalize
  • Achievements to unlock your true hairy potential

NOTE: This is a very short game. If you get stuck try holding the phone up to your face.

Originally created for #WineJam 2019

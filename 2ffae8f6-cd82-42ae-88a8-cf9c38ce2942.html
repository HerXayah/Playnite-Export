Description

Pilots, wake up!

You are awakened by a strange, godlike creature and your mission is to save the world from evil...

Do you have the skills to master a badass, adrenaline pumping twinstick-shooter?

- 22 levels
- 6 boss-fights
- 4 weapons
- Many different enemys
- Highscore system
- Singleplayer or 2 player coop
- 4 difficulty settings (beginner, advanced, expert, insane)
- Full gamepad support
- Mouse/keyboard controlls
- 1080p Resolution
- Many different music tracks

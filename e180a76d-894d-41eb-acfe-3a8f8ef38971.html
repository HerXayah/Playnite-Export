Description

It’s all fun and games until someone ends up in Hell!

DemonsAreCrazy is an action-packed MOBA about a different type of electoral race, where demons vie for the title of President Satan.

Choose from 18 characters and gain notoriety by wreaking havoc and/or destroying everything in your path. Customize your demons’ skills, craft horrifically powerful equipment, and dive head-first into boss-hunts and quests – giving birth to the most evil, most deranged political party in history. Step right up – and enjoy Hell’s first election!

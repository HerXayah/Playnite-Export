game Information

i'm Keyboard, You're Mouse!
Let's be actors and cameraman and cooperate with friends!

P1 uses the keyboard to manipulate the actor
P2 uses the mouse to manipulate the cameraman

The actor has to jump over buildings, avoid obstacles, and film without a hitch.
But the cameraman shouldn't miss the actor

Controls

Cameraman : MouseControl
Actors : Keyboard (two arrow keys, and a jumpbutton)

You can set the keymapping in the settings

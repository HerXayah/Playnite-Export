Description

Stars Survivor is a totally DEMENTIAL multiplayer fighting game! Download the game and let yourself be captivated by its delicately crafted graphics, competitive gameplay and much more:

🔫 More Than 40 Different Equipment

💣 A Huge Map

🕹️ 1 Battle Royale Mode

🎮 1 World Ranking

🏰 Over 20 Different Planets You Can land On

💀 A Reward System When You Kills

👑 World Ranking 👑

Form a team with your friends, climb your world ranking to the top!
YOU MAY BE THE STRONGEST ON THE BATTLEFIELD BUT WILL YOU BE IN THE WORLD?

⚔️ Play together ... Or Against! ⚔️

In Stars Survivor EVERYTHING is possible! Play as a team so you can help your teammates destroy enemy ships, or against, fight 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, ... or even 4v4! So invite your friends and meet on the battlefield!

🗡️ Dozens Of Different Equipment🗡️

Stars Survivor features over 39 different weapons / gear you can practice your own! Want to use a more powerful ship, a thruster that goes faster, or even a laser cannon that does more damage? Go for it ! ALL IS CUSTOMIZABLE IN STARS SURVIVOR!

👾 Game Mode 👾

In Teams, Solo, Duel, ... So many opportunities to test your combat skills!

🎲 Mini-games 🎲

No internet connection? So launch the mini-offline from the menu! A mini-game in which you have to avoid the asteroids.

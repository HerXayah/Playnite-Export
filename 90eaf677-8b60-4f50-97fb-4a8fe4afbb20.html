Description

Plot overview

For thousands of years, the Yokai-girls in the "Country of Yamato" lived in peace with human beings, but the Yokai-girls suddenly rioted, and the whole world lost its color. As the "Master Hentai"before, you undertake the responsibility of saving the Yokai-girls and the world!

Features

  • Move the color blocks, complete the color arrangement puzzles, and reveal the truth of the riots!
  • 14 Yokai-girls with different characters are looking forward to meeting you！❤
  • With live2D technology, all characters will move! Every Yokai-girls has multiple interactions!
  • Each character has an exciting personality voice!❤

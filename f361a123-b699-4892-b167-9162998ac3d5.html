Description

Shu's Misfortune is a sample RPG made in RPG Maker MZ by Moonana, the creator of the quirky RPG Virgo vs the Zodiac.

Shu wants to end her father's haute couture legacy by unraveling his empire of fashion. Shu is determined to embroider a most treacherous scheme that will put an end to this empire of threads and drought.

Features:
  • Challenging ATB combat where all decisions must be made quickly and swiftly.
  • Interact with all objects you can see in this tightly knit desert kingdom.
  • Around 1 hour long journey through what was once a fashionable society.
  • Lots of secrets, items and quests to be discovered.
  • Charming friends and foes to meet along the way.
  • One bonus track made by Elektrobear!

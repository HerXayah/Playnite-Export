Description

Enjoy the most famous paintings from Finland in Virtual Reality! The Art Gallery is placed on a space station, which additionally for the basic gallery, includes a warehouse and an extra gallery for Mr. Hugo Simberg's paintings.

Here is the list of painters in an alphaphetical order:

Ahlstedt, Fredrik
Churberg, Fanny
Edelfelt, Albert
Gallen-Kallela, Akseli
Järnefelt, Eero
Schjerfbeck, Helene
Simberg, Hugo
Wright, Ferdinand von
Wright, Magnus von

Description

Dolphin Swim
A simple experience in seeing how long you can last navigating a zig zag path.


It's all about FOCUS!
Whether you want to be a musician, pro gamer, or even a developer and more!
You're going to need a lot of focus!

Dolphin Swim aligns your brain's focus with a creative zigzag game!
Playing Dolphin Swim every morning can get your brain ready to reach your dreams!
Your score is your brain's focus strength for the day. A strength above 100 isn't necessary.
Dolphin Swim can even be a stress test for some people.

~Work your way up to a score of 100 everyday!~

Some Focus examples
Focus Speed 1: Attentive - 100 focus

Focus Speed 2
House Chores 20 - 25 focus
Homework 35 - 50 focus
Out of Bed 10 - 15 focus
Your Dreams 90 - 100 focus

Focus Speed 3
Reaction - 3 focus
Precision Timing Storage - 6 focus
Technique Discovery - 10 focus

Events to earn new achievements come and go but return eventually at random.

Eventually you can earn all of the achievements!
Events may contain challenge mode achievements that are a little harder to earn than others!

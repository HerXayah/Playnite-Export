Description

“Kyiv from dusk till dawn” is the first immersive tour around Kyiv led be two city guides. Put on the Lenovo Explorer headset and immerse yourself with new experience by traveling to 6 unique locations in one of the most ancient European capitals. Join the rehearsals at the House of Recordings and at the Theatre on Podil, feel the ride at a Cycletrack, enter one of the biggest libraries in the world — the Vernadskii National Library of Ukraine, explore the park of innovations UNIT.City and hang out at Art-Zavod Platforma. Listen to the story and interact with objects at every location to get the full experience. It’s time to explore Kyiv in VR!

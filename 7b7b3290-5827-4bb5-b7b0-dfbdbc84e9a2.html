Description

Become the Ultimate Puzzler!

Battle against the clock or others and get the top score.

Game modes include:

* Solo
** Basic Mode - The basic Puzz/LR experience
** Timed Mode - Watch out for falling pieces
** Jigsaw - Sit back, relax, and solve

* Multiplayer modes
** Go Head-2-Head with other another Puzzler
** Play Solo vs Everybody and grab the high score on the scoreboard

Features cross-platform multiplayer, play against other devices even VR!

Description

World Warfare is an intense 3D, Real-time Strategy game where hundreds of players around the world command dozens of land, sea, and air units in World War II-inspired combat. The goal is conquest and supremacy. Master your tactics, study your enemy, develop your war economy, build your city, form your band of brothers, and conquer the battlefield!

The game includes:

*Real-time, PvP combat.
Players fight on 7-day maps that dynamically change.

*Command 40+ land, sea, and air units from WWII.
Build, train, and enhance authentic WWII vehicles and weaponry!

*3D, immersive battlefields.
Destroy dozens of buildings and vehicles with authentic graphics and sound effects on a massive, open field map!

*Leagues and Alliances
Team up and support your teammates with firepower, then take on the enemy together!

*Strategy
Plan your attacks and defenses by spying on your enemies!

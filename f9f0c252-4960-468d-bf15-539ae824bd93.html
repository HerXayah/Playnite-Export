Description

Get ready to Jump into a fun little adventure that might just take you to big places!

In Finding Fido: The Journey you're able to explore intricate environments filled with interesting characters to meet along your quest to save your dog!



Finding Fido: The Journey is a Third Person Adventure game featuring:

easy to Learn Controls and Interfaces

fun Levels to Explore

interesting Npcs to Interact With

a story That's Simple to Engage In

The only Content included in this free offering is the Finding Fido: The Journey Application.

expect A Fun and Easy Going Journey!

Finding Fido: The Journey is the first Steam released game from studio KanapaGames.

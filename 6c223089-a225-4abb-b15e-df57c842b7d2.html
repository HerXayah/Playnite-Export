Description

We aim to provide the most immersive and enjoyable representation of strategic 21st-century combat possible on the PC. Modern Combat caters to newcomers and veterans alike, and incorporates both traditional and 'Hardcore' damage systems.

  • Free Modification - Download and play at no cost whatsoever!

  • New Setting - Make the transition from 1940s Europe to current-day China. All-new factions, artwork, maps, effects and music help to bring a near-future Sino-American war to life.

  • Modern Warfare - Deploy helicopters, infantry fighting vehicles, MLRS trucks and digital reconnaissance vehicles. Fight the enemy with guided missiles, remote-controlled machine guns, automatic grenade launchers, suppressors, thermal goggles, ghillie suits, targeting lasers and more.

  • Faithful CoH Gameplay - A new spin on familiar Victory Point and Annihilate multiplayer game-modes with all the strategic depth that CoH has to offer. Try our Hardcore modes for more realism, or our custom campaign mission for a new singleplayer experience.

  • Competitive Focus - Designed with high-level competition in mind, Modern Combat strives for multiplayer balance.

  • Communal Roots - Driven by community feedback and funded entirely by community donations, Modern Combat is a project by players, for players. Visit our forums to provide feedback, and join our Steam group to meet fellow players.

Known Issues



  • To avoid game-breaking bugs, players must own all four CoH factions.

  • When loading a saved game, certain mod assets (such as weapons) may not load correctly.

  • Both US and Chinese troops employ existing US voice files. This is unfortunate (and immersion-breaking), but something we're currently unable to improve.

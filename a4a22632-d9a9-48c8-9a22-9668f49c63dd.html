Description

Create a snail and join the battle. Take control of the backyard with Battle Snails! Climb walls, hang upside down and move anywhere. Team up with your friends for a tank game unlike any other!

Meet the team

RocketSnail
Lance Priebe (aka RocketSnail) has been developing worlds since he was a child. In 2000 Lance created his first virtual world called Penguin Chat, then in 2005 he co-founded and created Club Penguin. Club Penguin quickly became one of the the largest Virtual World for Kids with more than 300 million players. Club Penguin was sold to the Walt Disney Company in 2007. Today, Lance continues to create new worlds for players of all ages to explore.

SkyVu
Ben Vu is the co-founder and CEO of SkyVu Inc. SkyVu was the first developer to publish a 3D mobile shooter on Android and the first developer to publish a cross platform real time PvP mobile shooter. With over 30 MILLION downloads of their award winning Battle Bears mobile game franchise. Battle Bears has reached the top of the charts in free, paid and top grossing in over 50 countries. Having previously worked on the Oscar nominated films Boxtrolls & Coraline and earning a Master's degree in Animation from CalArts, Vu has successfully combined high quality data driven free to play mobile games with original character franchises.

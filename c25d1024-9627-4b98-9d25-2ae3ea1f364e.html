Description

Welcome to Tricking 0 Alpha the first Tricking Videogame. Tricking is a sport that combines kicks with flips and twists from martial arts, gymnastics as well as many dance moves and styles from breakdancing. If you like martial arts, gymnastics, tumbling, parkour, free running, or movement and acrobatics in general, you will probably love the game. With a flick-it gameplay allowing you to perform hundreds of tricks, and combine them how ever you want, the possibilities are endless !!

100+ TRICKS


Use the Flick-it gameplay to Flip, Twist and Kick and Combine 100+ Tricks to express your own creativity.
You can aim for pure tricking style combos



Or combos inspired by gymnastics and tumbling

RAGDOLL PHYSICS


The game is challenging but if you fail you will be rewarded by nasty and goofy bails thanks to the Ragdoll physics !!!

REPLAY


The replay feature allows you to watch your best combos over and over

GAME MODES


The game contains a Free Mode, allowing you to practice your tricks and combos, and 2 multiplayer modes for you to challenge yourself and your friends on a local multiplayer, or against bots.
You can also use your kicks to destroy your friends and make them fail their combos !!!




The game is made and designed for gamepad, but you can fully enjoy the game with mouse and keyboard !!!

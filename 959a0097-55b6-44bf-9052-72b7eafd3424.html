Description



... Of Losers, Cool Kids and Gods, where Losers wish they were Cool Kids, Cool Kids wish they were Gods and Gods rule over all, Cool Kid Cody embarks on his quest for Godhood! All he's gotta do is achieve the impossible: transform the biggest Loser in school into a Cool Kid. Should he fail, he might just lose his cool.



Cool Kid Cody is an interactive point & click cartoon series!

Interact with the world using point & click mechanics!
Collect items, make choices and explore in order to progress the story!
Find bonus photos scattered across each episode!
Keep your ride powered up by making the coolest choice for the coolest scenes!



This game gives you free access to Episode 1: Cool Kid Cody's Day On! It’s God Recruitment Day and Cody’s late for class! He’d better jet if he’s gonna impress Crescent High’s almighty God!

Check out the DLC for later episodes. Season 1 was released from April 8th, 2022 - June 10th, 2022. Season 2 will be released from April 7th, 2023 - June 9th, 2023. Follow this page or our social media to keep up with updates!

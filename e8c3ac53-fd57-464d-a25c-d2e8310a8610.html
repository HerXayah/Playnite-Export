Description

Isorropia is currently in the alpha stage of development

Isorropia Is built to level the skills of players by auto-balancing the power of your characters. This allows players of different skill levels to have a fair chance at a competitive multiplayer fighting game.
There are five rounds of arena-style combat with four rounds of upgrading between. You will receive upgrade points proportionally to the enemy player's score.
The goal of the game is to have the highest score at the end. The difficulty comes with balancing how many upgrades you need to achieve combat superiority by the end.

  • 2- 4 local multiplayer
  • 22 upgrades
  • Arena-style combat
  • Top-down strategy fighting

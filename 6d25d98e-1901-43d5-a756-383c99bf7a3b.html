Description

This is a fun online multiplayer battle royal game, you can play against your friends and try to get the epic victory. In this game, you drop out of a plane and fight each other until only 1 remains alive, try to stay alive while the deadly zone closes in. Do keep in mind, that this game is still in the early stages of development and therefore there will be loads of updates and changes.

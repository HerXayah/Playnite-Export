Description


POLYGON is a tactical multiplayer team 1st person shooter.
Using various weapons, you will battle with up to 32 players on large-scale maps.
Properly managing your arsenal and skills, you will lead your team to victory!



Team up with up to 16 players to fight in furious PvP matches.
Group up with your friends in the squad to bring the teamplay to another level!



A large arsenal is available – each weapon has a real prototype!
Choose between assault rifles, PDWs, LMGs, DMRs and Sniper rifles to find your play style.



A customization system will help you to adjust your weapon to your needs.
Change sights, handles, muzzle devices and accessories.
Also, you can unlock many skins to show off on a battlefield!



Jump into battle in any location: from spacious valley to narrow subway.
Different maps – different situations!

The game is constantly being improved.
Change the outcome of the battle using new weapons and skills.
Team up with friends and explore new locations.

DEVELOPMENT


Intuitive controls and obvious gameplay make the game simple and fun.
POLYGON can be run on outdated PCs and show all its all capabilities on modern computers – thanks to the Unreal Engine 5.
Launched by cutting-edge innovations, the Unreal engine gives the POLYGON game amazing graphics!

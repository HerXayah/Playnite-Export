Description


You wake up in a crappy and very strange place, like an old factory or warehouse. You are locked in one of the rooms. You don't know how you got in here and what to do next. Suddenly, an unfamiliar and arrogant voice from the loudspeaker orders to obey him and follow the rules in order to survive and get out of here. Everywhere around is semidarkness, confusion, and loud clinking sounds are emitted. The locals know you're here. Death is inevitable if inactive, but outside the room is also dangerous. There is only one option, to move unnoticed and avoid encounters with monsters, because health is limited.

Sogo Vego: The Untold Story
After the events of the original game, toy will play for a new hero, for Mishka. His tasks on the basis are very trivial: collect keys, spread notes, establish contact with monsters. He is a little helper who does small but important work. However, this time something changes. The monsters at the base have become smarter. The tester poses questionable tasks. Subject has escaped from the base. A new friend appears. In new conditions, you have to find out how it all began. In this story, you will find out what is happening behind the screen. In the soup of events, you will become a hero, not just another victim. You are the one who will put an end to what is happening.



Features:

  • Realistic monsters, with their past.
  • A short but exciting story.
  • Some monsters are quiet, so surprise is guaranteed.
  • Nice soundtracks.

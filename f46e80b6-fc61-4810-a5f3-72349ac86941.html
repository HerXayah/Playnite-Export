Description

4Prot is an interactive prototyping laboratory that allows you to create virtual spaces for students and professionals to creatively build different types of prototypes.

The free version of 4Prot allows you to save 2 projects with 100 blocks each. It also has 2 paid versions: Student Pack and Professional Pack.

4Prot is based on the construction with tiles and "Blocks" type objects that allow the user to easily build prototypes of processes and 3D models according to their needs.

It has a functional menu that allows you to choose between more than 1000 blocks or bricks, which can be modified in their position, color, size, etc. characteristics.

Create your own animated presentation with multiple scenes, add smart cameras and actors to the presentation.

Set up your work team and create projects in online multiplayer sessions with your Steam friends.

4Prot allows you to tune academic content with the technological tools that students use outside the classroom.

It is ideal for collaborative work with students and university professors.

You can create prototypes for international trade, logistics, budgets, accounting, industrial engineering, civil engineering, occupational safety, marketing, jurisprudence or any other area of ​​knowledge.

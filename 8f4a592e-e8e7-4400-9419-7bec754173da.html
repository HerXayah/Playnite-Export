Description

Five-Finger Gunslinger is a third-person online arena-battle game where players compete against their friends to become the most popular kid in the west. Battle evil cactus-bandits and pelt your opponents with water balloons as you struggle for suburban supremacy!

Features

  • A handcrafted western neighborhood created by kids to live out their wild west fantasies!
  • Three unique characters: Five-Finger Gunslinger, Rancher Enchanter, and Astronaut Aquashot each with unique gameplay and mechanics!
  • Persuade kids to join your posse by spending coolness points collected from defeating imaginary cactus-bandits!
  • Grow your posse to raise your popularity! Increase your popularity to grow your popularity-sphere and assert your dominance over the neighborhood!
  • Three types of imaginary cactus-bandits to battle: Disaster Cactus, Shadow Cactus, and Crystal Cactus!
  • Move inside other player's popularity-spheres to see what enemies they are imagining!
  • Destroy your opponent's enemies and steal coolness away from them!
  • Play with friends in private servers, online against strangers, or even battle vs AI opponents!

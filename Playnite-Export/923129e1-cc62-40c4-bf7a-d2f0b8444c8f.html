“Hidden Dream” is a adventure puzzle game where players can explore and gather collectables. In this game, players experience story of how Princess Wencheng undergo her journey to Tibet to marry Srongtsen Gampo in Princess Wencheng’s fantasy world.
There are an abundant amount of puzzles and also a lot of collectable elements in this game. The collections include traditional history and culture, daily necessities, local customs and natural scenery of China in the Tang Dynasty. Players will experience diverse game mechanics and an emotional journey through this wonderful adventure that Hidden Dream carries.



“Hidden Dream” is a journey that is full of mysteries and thought provoking moments. Players will play as Princess Wencheng and experience her change gradually in her fantasized dream world.

Players will explore a new world by learning a variety of tools step by step, slowly resolving the frustration within Princess Wencheng. Players will grow with Princess Wencheng while going through this rough journey.

Players will experience traditional Chinese music, great scenery across China presented with our stylized graphics , the fun of solving puzzles, and the richness of Chinese traditional culture and history through the bits and pieces of the story in the game.

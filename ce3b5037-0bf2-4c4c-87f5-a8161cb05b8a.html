Description



You have to restore order in a world where there is a struggle for valuable information. Corporations have gained their independence and commit atrocities everywhere. Firstly, you and artificial intelligence Shi need to restore the Triple-A base and engage in hiring agents to complete missions.

Twilight Wars: Declassified is a free demo version of the Twilight Wars game. It consists of one mission called «Signal from the Past». After this mission you will know the world of the game better. You should find out who is transmitting the signal. Ally? Or is it an ingenious ploy of the enemy? A difficult path awaits you, which you can go through in different ways, using the individual abilities of agents.





  • Dungeon Crawler in the unusual setting of spy cyberpunk: tactical turn-based combat, dangerous missions and irreversible death of agents in the cruel world of the near future.
  • Original art style: immerse yourself in the atmosphere of lo-fi cyberpunk.
  • The power of words: it's not just about the decisions you make on the battlefield; it's also about the stances you take during interactive dialogues with various characters.



  • A squad for every playstyle: there are 8 unique agents with their own strengths and weaknesses, a flexible progression system and skill synergy between specialists. Choose from hundreds of possible combinations to build the squads that work best for you.
  • Think ahead as you embark on missions because they are closely interconnected. The choices you make during these missions will have a direct impact on the difficulty of global missions and the value of the rewards you earn. Whether you decide to take risky decisions or proceed with caution, it's crucial to keep in mind that a mistake can come with a high cost.
  • Every agent is an individual: by monitoring their stress and loyalty levels, considering their fears and quirks, and acknowledging the dynamics withing the squad, you can prevent mission failures, specialists leaving the Agency, or even casualties.



  • The old guard on guard: recruit 8 legendary agents from the 'Mediator' special squad, and utilize their unique abilities.
  • In the cyberpunk era, energy is as vital as air. It's crucial to ensure that your agents have enough batteries to recharge before going on a mission. Without adequate power, their weapons and implants may shut down, and some skills will become inaccesible.

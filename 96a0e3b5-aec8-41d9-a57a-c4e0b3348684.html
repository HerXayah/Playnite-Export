Description

*ALL WARBAND & COSMETIC DLC NOW INCLUDED IN THE MAIN GAME!*



Warhammer Underworlds – Shadespire Edition is a digital adaptation of the explosive, high-stakes, turn-based strategy tabletop game from Games Workshop that pits mighty warbands from the Age of Sigmar universe against one another in an eternal player versus player battle for Glory. Choose a warband, build your decks and carve a path to victory using dice-and-card mechanics that offer boundless strategic depth.

ASSEMBLE YOUR WARBAND

Trapped within the towering spires of the Mirrored City of Shadespire, fourteen warbands battle it out for survival - each with unique abilities and a distinct combat style. All of these warbands are now available to play in online matches, bot matches or single-player challenges, having previously been available as DLC!

Playable warbands:

Steelheart’s Champions - Magore's Fiends - Garrek’s Reavers - The Sepulchral Guard – Spiteclaw’s Swarm – The Farstriders – The Chosen Axes – Ironskull’s Boyz – Thorns of the Briar Queen – Eyes of the Nine – Drepur’s Wraithcreepers – The Storm of Celestus – Stormsire’s Cursebreakers – Mollog’s Mob

CRAFT YOUR DECK

Your success is measured in Glory points - awarded for achieving objectives and vanquishing your foes. Deckbuilding is the strategic heart of Warhammer Underworlds – Shadespire Edition. Your 20-card power deck is filled with clever gambits, powerful upgrades, and cunning spells you’ll need to inflict maximum damage. Your 12-card objective deck will shape your tactical approach to a match, allowing you to score Glory by fulfilling specific conditions.

CLAIM THE SPOILS OF SHADESPIRE


A rewarding progression system in both online matches and single-player Challenges allows you to earn cosmetic content including warband skins, deck portraits, and player titles! Only those who have proven themselves in the unforgiving streets of Shadespire will be able to claim these rewards.

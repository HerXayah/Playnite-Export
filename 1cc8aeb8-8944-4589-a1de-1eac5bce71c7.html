Description

Conquer the universe

In Grocket you fly through space with a rocket and try to get to the safe base.
Immerse yourself in an exciting and dangerous journey in space that challenges you and your concentration in its own way.
There are numerous space hazards such as obstacles, magnetic zones and wormholes, as well as lasers,
lurking by the wayside. You have to get control of time and solve numerous puzzles to continue this journey.
Break the laws of gravity and search for clues to the next stop on your journey.

Play with friends

Compete with your friends for time and exclusive rewards. Compete against them in leaderboards and secure your victory.
Don't have your own computer? No problem, you can play together on one device and try to break the laws of physics.

Features

• Storymode, where you get to know the different kinds of challenges and unlock new mechanics in the game.

• Freeplay, where you try to survive random challenges and put your skills with the new mechanics to the test.
• Co-op (Local Multiplayer) [beta: more levels coming soon]
• Steam Leaderboard
• Steam Achievements
• Steam Stats
• Shop and Skins

Description

In a world guided by a chaotic and destructive nature, the Druids order, defenders of balance, are going through a rough time. The Druid acceptance tests have begun, and young Oona is the new candidate. She will have to face 13 different trials in the span of one year to transform into a full-fledged Druid, thus acquiring the responsibility and power to restore balance to the world.

Join Oona in her journey and enjoy the beautiful scenario of this 2.5D platformer game featuring adventures and puzzles.

In the current version you can:
  • Finish Oona’s first trial reaching the Souls Portal.
  • Travel through forest, swamps and even the Clouds Kingdom in the first levels of the game.
  • Fight against four types of enemies.
  • Solve the different puzzles of each region.
  • Master Oona's first abilities as a Druid apprentice.
  • Fight the first two bosses of the game. The Santa Compaña and the Meiga.

A fantastic adventure inspired by the Galician and Celtic myths and legends full of beautiful settings and challenging dangers.

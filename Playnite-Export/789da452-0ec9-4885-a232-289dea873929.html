Description

Cats! Knives! Two of our timeline's greatest inventions are now paired together, but now the cats are using them to battle against each other!

In this 2D multiplayer action game, take control of these cats using the various sharp weaponry at your disposal and your cat-born agility to traverse the wide range of stages.

Features

  • 2 to 4 player local multiplayer OR online through Steam Remote Play!
  • Several different weapons to stab and throw with!
  • 15 different levels to play on!
  • 30+ different hats to equip your cat with!
  • A meow button!
  • Cats!
  • Knives!

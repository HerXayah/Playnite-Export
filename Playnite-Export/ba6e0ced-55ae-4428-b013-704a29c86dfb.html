Description

Looking to improve as an FPS player?


Aim Lab has been developed as your personal trainer to help you get better - in more games - through curated aim training scenarios, improving your core FPS skills including flicking, tracking, speed, perception & cognition through detailed aim analysis feedback which guides you on quick improvements. Aim Lab is regularly updated with new features to support more games and is a must for any players, from beginner to pro, to gain a competitive edge.

Reasons to train:


  • Official VALORANT VCT training partner
  • Official Rainbow Six Siege development partner
  • Over 12,000 training tasks & scenarios
  • Detailed aim analysis with improvement tips
  • Create unlimited maps, tasks & scenarios in Creator Studio
  • Over 500 FPS/TPS game profiles with a sensitivity convertor to match your settings including VALORANT, Apex Legends, CS:GO, R6S, Call of Duty & more.
  • Test your skills on our leaderboards against other players & dive into our special events
  • Track your improvements over time with your personalized dashboard
  • Great for warming up before hitting ranked!

Train your way:


  • Customize your training arena, targets, crosshair, sounds & more
  • Controller & keyboard / mouse support
  • 20+ weapons to train with or create your own!
  • Custom Recoil Patterns

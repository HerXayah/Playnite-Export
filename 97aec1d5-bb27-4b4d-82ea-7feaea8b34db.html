Description

Neon Boostis a freneticFirst Person Platformerbased on mechanics likeRocket-Jumpor Wall-Run. Run across3 different worldsto get the best times!

Run, Wall-Run, Slide, and most importantly, use your weapon to Rocket - Jump and get over the obstacles!

Features

  • 72 levels (36 standard levels + 36 "Lights Off" variants)
  • Alternative Game Mode:Lights Off
  • An original Synthwave-inspired soundtrack
  • Achievements!

Play on 3 different worlds:

  • Laser City
  • Tesla World
  • Cosmic Highway

Game Mode: Lights Off

Each level has an alternative version where you have to reach the goal with limited visibility.

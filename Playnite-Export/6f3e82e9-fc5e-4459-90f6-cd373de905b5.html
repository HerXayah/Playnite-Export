Description


In the game Fairy of the treasures you have to help a young fairy Mei to open magic chests and hidden gems, and gold! The game is a clicker with elements of visual novel.

Features:
- Interesting story about fairies and treasure.
- Fun and easy gameplay.
- Is not only a clicker, but also a visual novel! Go to story mode to get acquainted with the short story of the Mei and Kairo.

In total the game has 5 types of chests:

Golden chest


Sapphire chest


Emerald chest


Ruby chest


Ametist chest

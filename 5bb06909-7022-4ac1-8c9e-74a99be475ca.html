Description

InnerSpace is an exploration flying game set in a world where physics are inverted. Take the role of an unnamed Cartographer to explore the Inverse, a collection of worlds where the Ancients once lived. Collect relics, and encounter each world's mythical demigod, in a quest to discover the secrets of the Inverse, its history, and its future.

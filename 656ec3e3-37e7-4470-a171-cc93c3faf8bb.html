Description

You are now the DJ of Ouzo 54, a small, Greek-owned, Miami disco. But business has been going down lately...

And this is where you come in: Grab your vinyl packs, check your customers’ moods and play the correct music to keep them dancing - and paying!

The more money you make, the better disc packs you can buy. Or power-ups. Or beautiful props for your disco. And the more money you spend, the more your customers will pay you back!

Things you can do:


Be the DJ
Play your vinyls according to your customers' moods. Some like it chill and some like it hardcore. But be careful! if you make them angry, it will be a mess...

Upgrade your gear
How you’re going to spend your hard-earned money is up to you. Better Vinyl Packs, Power-Ups or DJ Props? Level up your gear and take your crowd to even higher scores!

Add your own music
You wanted a Heavy Metal disco? Or a K-pop one? Simply import your own music files and make the game completely yours.

Free Play
Just play some music and enjoy the Disco Destruction experience without any time constraints.

And remember: Never let the music stop!
